ABOUT US

TELITI INFOTECH SDN BHD, established on May 21, 2015, is a trusted leader in Malaysia's IT industry. Registered with the Malaysian Ministry of Finance, we specialize in delivering End-to-End Solutions, Including the Supply, Installation, Testing, Commissioning, Maintenance, and Servicing of Computer and Network Systems. Our diverse clientele includes government bodies, agencies, government-linked companies, financial institutions, and private businesses across Malaysia.

The name 'TELITI', inspired by the Malay word for "Meticulous" and "Skillful", embodies our business philosophy of precision and excellence. This commitment has earned us a reputation as one of Malaysia's premier providers of IT solutions and services.

At TELITI INFOTECH, we are dedicated to maintaining the Highest Standards of Quality, Reliability, and Innovation in every endeavor.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to be the trusted and preferred provider of Information Technology Solutions.

We aim to serve as a one-stop center, delivering comprehensive products and services tailored to meet our clients' needs.

We are committed to becoming a leading domestic company by expanding our regional business network, consistently delivering exceptional value, and upholding our industry leadership.
